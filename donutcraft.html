DonutCraft

Public ATLauncher Pack

DonutCraft is a 1.12.2 modpack. That includes everyone's favorite mods. This pack was built to accommodate all play styles, but with a twist. Many recipes have been changed to enhance and elongate your DonutCraft experience. This will allow you to spend months on this pack trying to reach the final goals, like creative flight and much more. We are constantly updating and making changes to the pack to give you the best experience.
